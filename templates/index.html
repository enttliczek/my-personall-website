About Me

Hello, I'm Kamil, a passionate and skilled baker with a strong background in both culinary arts and programming. My journey began with a Head Chef Degree, providing me with a solid foundation in the art of crafting delicious and high-quality baked goods.

Throughout my career, I have honed my skills in recipe development, team leadership, and inventory management. I take pride in maintaining the highest standards of quality, hygiene, and safety in the kitchen. My creative touch extends to dough shaping and the artful decoration of baked goods, ensuring a visually appealing and delightful experience for customers.

On the technical side, I have ventured into the world of programming. I am proficient in Python, with a focus on automation and problem-solving. My expertise includes web scraping, utilizing tools like Selenium, and developing web applications using Flask and Jinja2. I have also explored the data science stack, incorporating Numpy, Pandas, and Matplotlib into my toolkit.

With a knack for building RESTful APIs and designing responsive user interfaces with Bootstrap, I bring a unique blend of baking artistry and technical innovation to the table. My commitment to continuous learning is evident in my pursuit of expanding my programming skills through relevant courses.

Whether it's crafting the perfect pastry or writing efficient code, I thrive on challenges and collaboration. I look forward to bringing my passion for baking and programming to new opportunities and contributing to the success of innovative projects.
